Welcome to Ninja Hands

Prepare to embark on a thrilling journey into the world of ninjas with Ninja Hands - the ultimate action game where you harness the power of stealth, agility, and martial arts to overcome your enemies and emerge victorious.

Master the Art of Stealth

In Ninja Hands, you'll navigate through challenging levels filled with obstacles and adversaries, using your ninja skills to remain undetected and outmaneuver your foes. Sneak past guards, evade traps, and strike with precision to accomplish your missions.

Unleash Deadly Ninja Techniques

As you progress in Ninja Hands, you'll unlock a variety of deadly ninja techniques and weapons to enhance your combat abilities. From throwing shurikens with pinpoint accuracy to executing lightning-fast strikes with your katana, the power of the ninja is at your fingertips.

Complete Thrilling Missions

Embark on an epic adventure as you undertake daring missions and face off against formidable adversaries. From infiltrating enemy strongholds to rescuing hostages, each mission in Ninja Hands promises heart-pounding excitement and adrenaline-fueled action.

Challenge Your Friends in Multiplayer Mode

Gather your friends and compete in thrilling multiplayer battles in Ninja Hands. Test your ninja skills against players from around the world in intense PvP matches, or join forces with allies to take down powerful bosses and claim epic rewards.
